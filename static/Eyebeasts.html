Eyebeasts

16th July 2019 at 7:42am
Species WIP
  • Native to Síagh
  • Reclusive scholars, generally humanoid but with varying numbers of eyes–ranging from 1 to 20
  • Extreme distrust of humans, though more friendly towards Síaghans/Síaghan descendents
  • Rumored they’re descended from a group of Síaghans that were trapped outside the cities during the Tiercian Era and corrupted with suzi influence
  • Capable of bonding to a suzi companion and utilizing willpowers
  • Looks down upon any child born with exactly two eyes, or one that does not exhibit above average intelligence
  • Usually these outcasts go to integrate themselves into human societies
